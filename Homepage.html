Metro Cab offers handicapped accessible vehicle transportation including wheelchair accessible vans. We also have time-scheduled service for those patients of the City's hospitals and medical centers who require such service. We offer voucher account billing services for those medical facilities that may require a monthly or weekly billing.
Metro offers 24-hour transport service to Logan International Airport. For your convenience, you may schedule a transport to Logan 24 hours in advance. Just call our radio room at 617-782-5500 and one of our operators will assist you.
Metro Cab offers same day, expedited delivery service for your time sensitive packages and envelopes. Call our general manager at 617-787-5438 to obtain custom delivery rates.
Uniquely, unlike other taxi-cab companies, Metro Cab of Boston provides "call-out" service for all taxi customers. This will enable you to stay indoors during inclement weather, and be called out upon the taxi's arrival.
